About Me

Let me tell you about myself.

Hi! My name is Justin Heath. I'm 19 years old and I'm from Escondido, California. I lived in Idaho Falls, Idaho for 13 years, and currently go to college at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah.

Two of my favorite hobbies are film and music. I am currently trying to minor in Media Arts at BYU because of my growing love for film over the last few years, and I work as a cameraman at BYUtv. Back home I have a band with my brothers. Each of us know how to play 5 or more instruments, so we decided to make a band. We call ourselves Space Donut. Our music can be seen in the video below, and we even have an album on Spotify.

I also really enjoy surfing and basketball. Basketball comes more naturally to me due to the fact that I am 6 feet and 5 inches tall, so it has been a love of mine most of my life. Surfing, on the other hand, was something I was scared of for a long time. When I lived in Idaho we would visit San Diego fairly often, and my dad always wanted to teach me, but I was afraid. One day, I decided to conquer my fear, and I fell in love with it. While I still am not very good, I enjoy doing it, and that's really all that matters. While not doing either at the moment, I have been having fun playing intra-mural inner tube water polo, me and my friends just won the championship!

I have experience and skills in serveral areas, those can be found here on my resume.
